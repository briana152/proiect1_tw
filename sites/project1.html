ATTACK ON TITAN

Rating:💛💛💛💛💛/5

SEASON NR. OF EPISODES RELEASE DATE
season 1 25 episodes april 7, 2013
season 2 12 episodes april 1, 2017
season 3 22 episodes july 23, 2018
season 4 28 episodes december 7, 2020

One of the best animes I have ever watched. The story in the beginning is pretty simple and easy to follow, basically humans vs titans, but it gets more complex with every episode. It`s a show that keeps ur interest peaked all the time with all the mystery and action. The more u learn about the characters and the world they live in, u start asking urself a buch of questions either about the show itself, or life in general. The show touches on a lot of serious topics later on so it encourages critical thinking. It`s not something u can just put on as background noise. U miss one minute, u miss a whole plot cause the anime is filled with little spoilers hidden in whatever might be going on in the background, dialog, openings or endings etc. It is also an emotional rollercoster😩. WATCH IT!!👺

TOKYO GHOUL

Rating:💛💛/5

SEASON NR. OF EPISODES RELEASE DATE
season 1 12 episodes july 4, 2014
season 2 12 episodes january 9, 2015
season 3 12 episodes april 3, 2018
season 4 24 episodes september 29, 2018

This one is probably the biggest disappointment in anime history so I`m not gonna bother with it💀. First two seasons are amazing, 10/10, but it just gets really bad from there😬. I recommend u watch just those first two seasons and then continue with the manga, or just read the manga...or just dont acknowledge its existence at all😴. I`m pretty petty about this one cause it was going so well until they decided to pull one of those "the main character hit his head and completely lost all his memories"🙄 and because of that the whole story changes in the last two seasons. I still cry about this at night🤕. Moral of the story WATCH IT AT UR OWN RISK‼️

HAIKYUU

Rating:💛💛💛💛💛/5

SEASON NR. OF EPISODES RELEASE DATE
season 1 25 episodes april 6, 2014
season 2 25 episodes october 4, 2015
season 3 10 episodes october 8, 2016
season 4 25 episodes january 11, 2020

The best sport anime on god🙌🏼. It is the only sport anime I`ve watched BUT still the best one out there, trust me. It`s about this short guy who wants to become the best volleyball hitter. Since he obviously doesn`t meet all the required standards for that position we get to see all his struggles trying to become better day by day. We also get to see a lot of how all the other characters deal with their own problems (team work, lack of motivation, not being smart, talented or strong enough, feeling like ur incapable of doing anything right and all that). It really took me by surprise to see a sport anime be lowkey deep, or at least that`s how I see it. And on top of that it`s funny as shit and the animations are well done and accurate. MUST WATCH!💪🏼🏐

BOKU NO HERO ACADEMIA

Rating:💛💛💛💛/5

SEASON NR. OF EPISODES RELEASE DATE
season 1 13 episodes april 3, 2016
season 2 25 episodes april 1, 2017
season 3 25 episodes april 7, 2018
season 4 25 episodes october 12, 2019
season 5 25 episodes march 27, 2021
season 6 TBA 2022

I don`t hate it nor do I love it particularly; it is a decent anime. It`s about this kid who`s born without a quirk, in a world where most people have a quirk and him trying to become the no.1 hero. The protagonist feels pretty basic ngl and the story kinda follows the same pattern like most animes with an overpowerd mc. The idea is interesting but overall the anime can be cringe and boring. I woudn`t have minded the cringe and boring parts if it was at least funny but tbh I can`t even remember one time I laughed watching it😐. The memes are funny tho. It has very unique characters, the plots are great too but I fell like all that potential is being wasted since it tries to appeal to kids too much🤷🏽‍♀️. WATCH IT IF U DONT HAVE ANYTHING BETTER TO DO

KAICHOU WA MAID-SAMA!

Rating:💛💛💛💛💛/5

SEASON NR. OF EPISODES RELEASE DATE
season 1 26 episodes april 2, 2010

The first anime I watched and I love it🤩. It`s a romance anime...they`re all the same, BUT this one has to be my all time fav. Usually I can`t take romance seriously but what`s so good about this one is that it doesn`t try too hard. It kinda makes fun of the cliche things that happen in most romance anime. It is cringe but in a funny way. I just know I laugh every time I watch it. The anime is pretty cute too🤭. The bad side is that there`s only one season and the ending is kinda open so we don`t really get to see what happens with the protagonists but other than that everything is great🥳. ANOTHER MUST WATCH!😋
